Information

Take a look at the College Selector. Our tool will assist you to discover and discuss the complex decision to choose a school that fits your needs ... for about the price of a good lunch.

The College Selector is more than a list. It's an interactive template helping you to organise and prioritise your own selections.

Capture all your thoughts in one place and then graphically show results for your personal comparisons. Topics include groupings for academic life, real life and financial life helping you to research, and review up to 500 variables.

Then review your selected schools to see their balance of your individual academic and lifestyle needs. Plot the results using your own scores together with cost projections. You will be surprised to see illustrations that help you to determine what fits your personal needs and family’s objectives best.

In four simple steps, College Selector uses a spreadsheet-based template ready for use in typical desktop software. College Selector will help save you time and logically order your thoughts for up to 50 criteria applied to 10 potential schools. Start with a list, input scores for individual criteria, include estimates of your financial plans and review charts and graphs with your personalised results. Keep refining your model when you have new ideas and updated information; the results pages will continue to illustrate your latest insights.

Organising and graphically highlighting your thinking are important steps to help make complicated decisions. So, download the College Selector today and get back to what matters most ……… Finding the path to a university that is the right fit
